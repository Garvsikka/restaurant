The Cellar

Toronto's Most Intimate Private Dining Room

Hidden below The Harvest Table's main floor — a room that earns its discretion.

The Cellar was designed for the dinner you want to get exactly right. Fully private, with no shared walls and a dedicated kitchen pass, it seats up to twelve guests in a setting that feels more like a beautiful private home than a restaurant event space.

Our events team works with you in advance to build a bespoke seasonal menu — drawn from the same relationships with Ontario farmers and VQA vintners that define our main dining room, but crafted exclusively for your occasion.

The Cellar has hosted proposals, retirements, signings, anniversaries, and everything in between.

Planning Your Evening

Booking Lead Time

We recommend enquiring at least 3–4 weeks in advance for weekends, and 2 weeks for weekday evenings. The Cellar is limited to one booking per evening.

The Space

The Cellar seats 6–12 guests at a single long table. It is fully private with its own entrance, dedicated server, and private kitchen pass. Entirely separate from the main dining room.

Menu Process

After your enquiry, our events team will contact you to discuss your preferences, dietary requirements, and occasion. Chef Moreau designs a bespoke menu from there.
