Reservation Policy

We confirm by email within 24 hours. We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes. For parties of 6 or more, please call us directly at (647) 555-0183.

Private Dining

The Cellar seats up to 12 guests for private dinners, business meals, and special celebrations. Bespoke menus available. Contact us to enquire.

Walk-Ins

We reserve a portion of our dining room for walk-in guests each evening. Bar seating is also available without a reservation — first come, first served.
