Rooted in Ontario

Where Every Season Tells a Story

Handcrafted dishes built on relationships with Ontario farmers, vintners, and artisans.

We opened The Harvest Table with a single conviction: that a great meal begins long before the kitchen. It begins in the fields of Prince Edward County, the orchards of Grey County, and the vineyards stretching along the Niagara Peninsula.

Our menu shifts with the seasons — because the best ingredient is always the freshest one available from our neighbours who grow it with care.

Our Sourcing

The Provenance Behind Every Plate

We know our farmers by name. We visit their fields. We trust their craft.

Our proteins come from heritage-breed farms in Grey County. Our produce arrives weekly from market gardens in Prince Edward County. Our wine list celebrates the extraordinary VQA vintners of the Niagara Peninsula — because Ontario's terroir deserves its moment at the table.

No imported fillers. No flavour approximations. Just Ontario, at its honest best.
